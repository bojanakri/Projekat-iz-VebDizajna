Plot:

Cinderella is a folk fairy tale that contains elements of a myth about an unjustly oppressed person who triumphs. The main heroine is a young girl who lives a difficult life, which suddenly changes due to happy circumstances. In this fairy tale a girl named Cinderella stays with her father and mother, and continues to live with a mace and her terrible half-sisters. Cinderella was forced to work hardest at home, instead of her mace. One day, an invitation to the Royal Ball arrived in which it was said that all unmarried girls were called. Cinderella was allowed to go to the ball, provided she did all the work in the house. She gets to do all the jobs, but she does not wear a dress. But the thoughts and birds made her dress. Seeing these jealous half-sisters split her dress. Poor Cinderella reveals a good villa that helps her, but tells her that she will only last until midnight. The cinderella danced with the prince at the ball, forgetting her watch. When the clock had sounded at night, Cinderella fled forgotten a glass shoe. The prince ordered that all the girls try the shoes to find the Cinderella. When the order came that Pepeljuga pierced the shoe of the evil mace, she broke her shoes, but she had another with the Cinderella. The prince gave birth to Cinderella and lived happily until the death of them.
