Film Titanic winner 5th BJ FEST

Belgrade's winner for the best film was given to director James Cameron for the Titanic, according to the words of the jury ", a film that through a convincing story, as well as through the complex main character, harmonized all aspects of film aesthetics." Cameron personally received the award and thanked for this recognition, which means a lot to him as an incentive since this is his first film.
