Tonight is officialy ended Fest BJ in Sava Center

The last projection of the festival was the film The face of the Polish director Malgorzata Sumovska, and since the festival awards were awarded during the previous two festival nights, instead of the official closing ceremony, the art director Jugoslav Pantelic addressed the audience before the screening of I, Tonja in the Sava Center. He pointed out that in the previous ten days, 418 projections were held, 116 of them premiered, in 12 halls around the city, and that the festival was attended by over 70 guests. Pantelic then decided to greet all those who are deserving of what FEST is. "Usually, from evening to evening we welcome stars, guests, members of the jury, and now I beg you to greet a group of people without which FEST would not be," said Pantelic, and then invited the FEST team to join him on stage. The audience applauded the numerous team that worked at the festival. "Thank you colleagues, thank you Beograde for already 46th time together, and what we hope for in the coming years will be even better. We are already scheduling a meeting for the next year, the next, 47th FEST will be held from February 22 to March 3 2019, "added Pantelic. He then recalled the well-known Vlada Divljan who left us three years ago, adding that his song "Only one love I have" could have been devoted this evening to all of us who love the film.
