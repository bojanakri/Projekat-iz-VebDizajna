Plot:

Captain Nathan Algren, as a civil war hero, is coming to Japan to fight with the samurai, and maybe eventually he himself accepts their Codex and vow to them for loyalty. The other soldier saw his way of life as a slumber. He is Kacumoto, the last leader of the samurai, who is devote their lives to saving the rulers and the earth. However, times have changed as well as values. What the samurai lived and fought for it no longer exists. However, Kacumoto does not want to withdraw without a fight. The paths of the two warriors will merge.
