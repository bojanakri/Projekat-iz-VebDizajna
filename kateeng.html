Kate Winslet





Kate Elizabeth Winslet is an acclaimed English actress, winner of the Grammy Awards, BAFTA, and the Film Academy Award. She won her first Oscars in 2009, since she was nominated five times previously. Her debut film, Paradise Creatures, directed by Peter Jackson in 1994, gave her critical and audience acknowledgment and opened her doors to the film world. In this film, she acted as a lively and greasy teenager, who helped her friend kill her mother, for she defended her that the two of them were together. After that, new roles followed, and in 1997 and Titanic. The film earned large sums of money at the box office and even 11 Oscars. So far she has recorded 20 films. She set the record as the youngest actress nominated for the Oscars. Her acting partners are numerous, and among the most famous are Geoffrey Rush, Emma Thompson, Judi Dench, Leonardo DiCaprio Jim Carrey, and Johnny Depp. She offered her voice in several dashes, which had great success.
