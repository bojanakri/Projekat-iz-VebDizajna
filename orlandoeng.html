Orlando Bloom





Orlando Jonathan Blum (Orlando Jonathan Blanchard Bloom, Kenterberi, England, January 13, 1977) is a British film actor. Actors of Legolas were most prominent in the trilogy of the Lord of the Rings and Hobbit, and besides that role, he also had remarkable roles such as: Will Turner in three parts of Pirates of Caribbean, Beijlien in Heaven's Kingdom, and Paris in the Trojan Film Spectacle.
