Jury

Aleksandar Bercek

Aleksandar Bercek is a famous Serbian actor. Born in 1950 in Vrdnik. He has played roles in over 40 films and in numerous theater performances.
Roles: Getting a Dollar, Returning Unpaid, Professor Kosta Vujic's Hat.

Vojin Cetkovic

Vojin Cetkovic (Krusevac, August 22, 1971) is a Serbian actor. The FDU finished in Belgrade in the class of Professor Vlada Jevtovica.
Cast: Zona Zamfirova, Family Treasure, Nemanjica, Citrus for Eskobar, Wounded Eagle

Tanja Boskovic

She was born on June 27, 1953 in Belgrade. She has a rich TV and film career. She graduated from the Faculty of Dramatic Arts in Belgrade in 1973 in the class of Professor Predrag Bajcetica. Cast in: Balkan Expres, Wild Strawberries, Theater in the House.
